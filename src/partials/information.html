Important Information
(Preparing to fly)

All flights are one-way. If you are interested in a return flight, you will need to book two one-way tickets.

All pe ts must have an up-to-date rabies vaccine within the last 12 months, to fly. Note that all pets must have proof of rabies vaccination within 12 months to enter most countries, including Grenada.

Flights are offered on a first-come, first-serve basis, and your seat is secured once full payment is received.

We ask for the weight of all pets and hoomans to ensure proper balance on the aircraft.

A minimum of 50% of the seats must be sold for the plane to fly. If 50% of the seats are not sold within 30 days of scheduled departure, the flight will be cancelled, and you will receive a full refund of the ticket price via return to the method in which you paid.

You are allowed one (1) checked bag up to a maximum of 50 lbs. and one (1) carry-on bag up to a maximum of 20 lbs.

All dogs must be muzzled while in transit, including at all times while in the airplane and in airport terminals.

Note that all pets returning to the USA must have a crate that they can fit in upon landing in the USA for clearance. Pets returning to the USA from Grenada at this time do not need proof of rabies vaccination, but the CDC website should always be checked for a potential change in this regulation.
