How does it Work?

1

Pets fly in the cabin with their owners. Each hooman purchases a seat at the quoted one-way price, which allows one pet to travel. More than 1 pet? Contact us for a price quote.

2

Payment in full is due a minimum of 30 days prior to the flight. This is necessary to procure the flight. Once booked, tickets are 100% non-refundable. Do not book your ticket unless you are absolutely certain you are going to fly. In case of a delay of the flight due to mechanical problems on the plane, third party actors (e.g., problems with the airport) or Act of God, the flight will be rescheduled.

3

Once payment is confirmed, you will receive your e-ticket and flight confirmation via email. Note that payment in full is required to procure your seat. The email will provide all the information you need to prepare you (and your pet) for the flight.

4

Enjoy your flight!
