About Us

Hi there! Paws Air offers charter flight service between North America and the Caribbean for hoomans and their pets.

We believe pets are family, and should be treated that way. All our pawsengers travel in the main cabin with their hooman(s) on all our flights. Our planes hold between 20 and 100 hoomans with their pets, depending on departure airport and demand.

You can also contact us for the most up-to-date schedule of flights and pricing and for any questions you may have.

We also offer return flight service from the Caribbean to North America. Contact us for return flight pricing.

No two hoomans (or pets) are alike — we are here to assist you and ensure your flight is an absolutely pawsitive experience.

Mission

To get pets and their hoomans where they need to go, together.

Vision

Pets are family members (maybe with a little more fur) and should be treated that way.
