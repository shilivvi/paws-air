Our service

Each plane is configured for pets and hoomans and is professionally deep cleaned after every flight. Some flights land in Puerto Rico for refuelling and to let you and your pet stretch out and take a bathroom break.

Upon arrival, you and your pet are given VIP treatment, disembarking in a separate area from the rest of the commercial aircraft passengers.

Be prepared to provide the age, sex, color, and breed of your pet.

The Grenada Ministry of Agriculture and Lands requires registration at least 2 weeks prior to your arrival.

Note that all pets coming to Grenada must have proof of rabies and other relevant disease vaccination within the last 12 months and a health certificate issued by a licensed veterinarian, stating that the pet is disease free and in good health, within two (2) weeks of the flight.

The Grenada Veterinary Officer may be present at the port of entry to inspect any pet that is arriving in Grenada.
