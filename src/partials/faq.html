Frequently ask question

Everything you need to know right here at your fingertips. Ask questions, browse around for answers.

  • Flight costs vary depending on fuel rates and the demand on charter planes at the time of flying. Expect to pay $2,500 USD+ for a one-way ticket for you and your pet.

  • There are multiple factors that drive up the cost. First, we do not overload the plane, which makes the experience less stressful for pets and their hoomans. Second, we book smaller jets, which makes it more likely that the plane will be filled, and the flight will proceed. That means we cannot achieve nearly the same economy of scale of much larger planes with many more passengers (you would need 8-9 flight attendants on a typical commercial flight to reach the same passenger: attendant ratio as on our flights!). Third, our planes must undergo a thorough deep cleaning after every flight, as they are also chartered for non-pet flights (sad!). Finally, our planes are mostly empty on the return leg of the journey because of load demand at that time of year. We are working hard to address this imbalance, with the goal of passing those savings on to our loyal customers. Every dollar saved is another dollar spent on chew toys and pet treats, so we take this work very seriously!

  • We accept all major credit cards, as well as email transfers (sent to pawsairlines@gmail.com), bank transfers, and wire transfers. Contact us for information on bank and wire transfers.

  • We do not run regularly scheduled commercial flights, but rather specialized flights during high-demand periods. Thus, we need lead-time to procure the planes that will take to the furry skies. We need to secure the planes 30 days in advance, which is why we require booking and payment of seats at least 30 days in advance.
